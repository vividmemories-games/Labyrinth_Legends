# Labyrinth (Original) vs Labyrinth Legends

## Executive Summary

Although **Labyrinth Legends** is inspired by the classic
paper-and-pencil **Labyrinth** game, it has evolved into a fundamentally
different experience. The original focuses on deduction, hidden
information, and multiplayer interaction, while Labyrinth Legends is
designed as a premium single-player puzzle adventure with exploration,
progression, and modern mobile gameplay.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Original Labyrinth

The classic game is essentially a **deduction game**.

Typical gameplay:

    Move Left
    ↓
    Wall
    ↓
    Move Right
    ↓
    Empty Cell
    ↓
    Update your hand-drawn map

The maze itself is the puzzle.

Players compete while gradually reconstructing the labyrinth using logic
and observation.

Features include: - Hidden map - Treasure - Wormholes - Rivers -
Hospitals - Arsenals - Weapons - Multiple players

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Labyrinth Legends

Labyrinth Legends transforms the idea into a modern adventure.

Instead of asking:

> "Where are the walls?"

The player asks:

> "How do I solve this room?"

Environmental mechanics include:

-   Pressure plates
-   Rotating bridges
-   Moving walls
-   Ancient mechanisms
-   Lasers
-   Collapsing floors
-   Magical crystals
-   Hidden switches
-   Enemy patrols

The maze becomes the setting rather than the entire puzzle.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Gameplay Loop

## Original

    Move

    ↓

    Discover Wall

    ↓

    Draw Map

    ↓

    Repeat

Information gathering is the gameplay.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Labyrinth Legends

    Observe Room

    ↓

    Plan Route

    ↓

    Draw Path

    ↓

    Confirm

    ↓

    Character Executes

    ↓

    Solve Puzzle

    ↓

    Collect Treasure

    ↓

    Reach Exit

Planning becomes the gameplay.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Controls

## Original

    Up
    Right
    Left
    Down

One command at a time.

## Legends

The player draws the complete route before execution.

Example:

    → → ↓ ↓ ← ↑ → →

Watching the plan execute successfully (or fail) creates anticipation
and satisfaction.

# Visual Direction

Original: - Pencil and paper - Grid-based - No animation

Legends: - Premium 3D / isometric presentation - Ancient ruins - Dynamic
lighting - Animated traps - Atmospheric music - Monument Valley / Lara
Croft GO inspired quality

# Progression

Legends: - Hundreds of handcrafted levels - Procedural challenge mode -
Coins - Relics - Character skins - Daily challenges - Achievements -
Seasonal events - Unlockable biomes

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Story

Legends: - Ancient civilization - Lost Architects - Hidden relics -
Mystical technology - Explorers uncovering forgotten history

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Monetization

Original: - None

Legends: - Cosmetic skins - Hint system - Premium level packs - Optional
Battle Pass - Rewarded ads - Premium version

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Unique Selling Point (USP)

The defining mechanic is **Draw Your Fate**.

Instead of directly controlling the explorer:

    Start

    ↓

    Draw Route

    ↓

    Confirm

    ↓

    Watch Execution

Players succeed through planning rather than reflexes.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Recommendation

One concept from the original game should be preserved:

## Preserve the Feeling of Discovery

Rather than revealing every level immediately:

-   Use fog of exploration
-   Reveal nearby tiles gradually
-   Ancient maps reveal small regions
-   Relics expose hidden passages
-   Secret rooms reward exploration

This keeps the spirit of the original paper game while modernizing every
other aspect.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Final Vision

**Original Labyrinth** - A multiplayer deduction game.

**Labyrinth Legends** - A premium puzzle adventure built around
planning, exploration, environmental puzzles, progression, and
discovery.

The result is not a remake of the original, but a modern evolution
inspired by its strongest idea: uncovering the unknown.
