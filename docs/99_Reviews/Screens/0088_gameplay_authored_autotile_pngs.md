## Task Summary

Replaced procedurally painted environment tiles (0087) with **16 authored AI-generated PNG autotiles** at 256×256, using Human-provided ChatGPT-style prompts (floor slab + impassable wall block + directional raised-edge variants). Wired `WallEdgeMask.autotileKind` to select a **single** sprite per walkable cell (`tile_floor`, `tile_n/e/s/w`, corners, `tile_ns/ew`, inner corners); blocked cells use `wall_fill`. Updated asset paths in `LLGameplayAssets`, restricted `GENERATE_GAMEPLAY_ASSETS` to sprites only (no tile overwrite). Out of scope: gem/portal/HUD sprite regen, T-junction (3-wall) perfect autotile art, cross-tile style consistency pass by art director.

## Motivation

Human rejected procedural `CustomPainter` tiles as low quality (“don't paint the tiles”). Requested high-quality PNGs matching original ChatGPT prompts: seamless dark temple floor, raised dark stone barriers, and 12–15 directional autotile variants. Single-sprite autotiling avoids the overlay corruption seen in 0087 (dark L-shapes on paths).
