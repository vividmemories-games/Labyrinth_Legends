## Task Summary

Human feedback iterations on Home logo placement: enlarged to near full-width, then nudged upward multiple times so the title sits in the sky band above the arch (not on the gate lintel). Final fix replaced `Stack` + percentage `Positioned` overlay with a single `Column` flow — currency row → logo (`LLSpacing.xs` gap) → `Spacer` → buttons/dock. Logo keeps `LLSpacing.sm` horizontal inset; all other Home visuals unchanged.

**Out of scope:** Logo asset regeneration, background swap, button styling, responsive breakpoint tokens.

## Motivation

**Why:** Percentage-based `Positioned` top values could not reliably pin the logo above the arch across devices; Human wanted the logo as high as possible without overlapping currency.

**Problem solved:** Logo consistently anchors directly below currency chips in the open sky area above the citadel arch.

**Approach:** Flow layout over absolute positioning — simpler, predictable, and matches mockup hierarchy (top bar → title → CTA stack).
