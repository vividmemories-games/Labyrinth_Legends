## Task Summary

Human requested floor visual variety on the code-drawn maze board: five authored floor PNGs (`tile_floor_1`–`tile_floor_4` plus `tile_floor.png` fallback), random per level attempt, per-cell rotation, and raised walls matching the adjacent walkable floor cell. Delivered: `FloorTileLayout` (seeded variant + quarter-turn assignment), texture loading for the variant pool, `MazePainter` drawing with rotation for floors and wall edges, seed propagation from `GameplayScreen` via `retryCount` (reshuffle on Try Again), slab lighting and subtle camera tilt retained from prior polish, tests, and `Gameplay.md` update. Out of scope: engine/schema changes, per-world theme UI, procedural tile generation, collectible/HUD sprite changes.

## Motivation

A single repeating floor texture made the board feel flat and synthetic despite the raised-stone wall renderer from 0090. Human supplied four variant slabs plus a canonical fallback. Per-attempt randomization with rotation breaks repetition without authoring per-level tile maps. Wall stones inherit the neighbor floor placement so vertical faces stay visually continuous with the slab they border.
