## Task Summary

Delivered an in-repo procedural gameplay asset pipeline and replaced broken ChatGPT tile art with LLDL-aligned PNGs. Initial pass added floor-centric wall overlays; Human QA flagged corrupted floor tiles (dark L-shaped patches on paths). **Amended:** removed runtime overlay stacking — each grid cell now renders a single full tile (`tile_floor` walkable, `wall_fill` blocked). Rewrote painters with fractal stone grain, gold inlays, cyan energy veins, and 512×512 export. Regenerated 14 environment PNGs plus explorer, key, lock, back, and settings sprites. `WallEdgeMask` and shadow overlay assets remain for future optional AO; not used at runtime. Out of scope: gem, portal, undo/erase/hint, trap_spikes regeneration; final art-director authored tile swap.

## Motivation

On-device QA showed (1) legacy tile PNGs as repeated mini-maze medallions, (2) in-maze/HUD sprites with excessive canvas padding, and (3) after first procedural pass, **wall edge overlays stacked on floor cells** producing unreadable dark shapes on paths. Human requested high-quality procedural tiles in-repo instead of waiting on external art.

**Approach:** `CustomPainter` → PNG export test (`GENERATE_GAMEPLAY_ASSETS=1`) keeps assets versioned and repeatable. One-tile-per-cell rendering matches top-down grid mockups and eliminates overlay corruption.
