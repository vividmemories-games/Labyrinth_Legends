## Task Summary

Authored **AB5 — AI Production Pipeline**, the operating specification for collaborative AI-assisted production. AB5 defines five collaborative production systems (Collaborative Philosophy, Collaborative Roles, Collaborative Workflow, Collaborative Quality, Collaborative Evolution) using a unified template — purpose, participants, responsibilities, workflow, quality expectations, authority, consistency rules, future evolution — while inheriting Vision, Gameplay, LLDL, AB0, AB1, and AB2–AB4 domain boundaries. **Out of scope:** prompt libraries, vendor manuals, model recommendations, gameplay, visual language, asset design, Flutter/git implementation, formal QA gates (AB6).

## Motivation

**Why:** AB0 established AI philosophy; AB1 established governance culture; AB2–AB4 defined domain production. The studio needed a tool-independent operating model for how humans and AI collaborate across documentation, assets, and implementation.

**Problem solved:** Prevents AI acceleration from becoming authority drift, unreviewed output, or parallel creative dialects; gives producers and contributors clear ownership and review discipline.

**Approach:** Extends AB0 §5 without redefining it; operationalizes AB1 governance; references AB6 for gate detail without anticipating it; tool- and model-independent language throughout.
