## Task Summary

Authored **AB4 — Marketing & Store Assets**, the production specification for public-facing brand communication. AB4 defines five communication systems (Brand Identity, Discovery & Store Presence, Campaign Communication, Community Communication, Brand Evolution) using a unified template — purpose, audience, communication goal, expectations, brand principles, contained assets, consistency rules, lifecycle — while inheriting Vision, Gameplay, LLDL/WS1, AB0, AB1, AB2, and AB3 (in-product brand boundary). **Out of scope:** marketing strategy, ASO, budgets, in-product UI, in-maze assets, implementation, legal policies.

## Motivation

**Why:** AB3 defined in-product interface and brand experiences. Prospective players and communities encounter the game through store listings, campaigns, and public updates — a separate production domain requiring honest expectation management and brand discipline.

**Problem solved:** Prevents misleading store imagery and campaign art that contradicts Draw Your Fate; gives publishing and creative contributors system-level briefs without inventing parallel marketing dialects.

**Approach:** One primary responsibility per communication system; explicit AB3/AB4 boundary (in-product authoritative, public extends); extends WS1 without redefining LLDL.
